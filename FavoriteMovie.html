My Favorite Anime is Naruto

Why is this my Favorite Anime

This is my favorite anime because this is the show that got me into anime. After the first episode I was hooked and watched the rest of the series. This is including the three shows and all of the movies. Naruto is one of the top animes to be made to this day.

Plot Summary

Naruto is about a ninja named Naruto Uzumaki who finds his path to become Hokage, the leader of the Hidden Leaf Village. The people of the village are feared by Naruto because there is a hidden beast within him, which destroyed the village once before. Naruto has to control the power within him to prove to the village that he can protect them and can be trusted. Sasuke is Naruto's best friend, rival, ally, and at times his enemy. Sasuke Uchiha is troubled with the massacre of his family clan and is sought out to avenge his clan. He does not have enough strength to seek revenge, so he leaves the village to gain more strength. Sasuke has gone astray and becomes Naruto's enemy. Naruto wants to bring back Sasuke to the right path and save him from his own hatred.

Dates Released, Ended, and Continued

The manga, which is the book was released on September 21st, 1999. The anime was released on October 3rd, 2002. The anime ended on February 8th, 2007 and has 220 episodes. When Naruto ended, Naruto Shippuden began shortly after and Naruto Shippuden is Part 2 of the manga, as well as a continuation of Naruto. The first episode of Naruto Shippuden aired on February 15th, 2007. The anime has 500 episodes and the last episode was aired on March 23rd, 2017.

Creators

Masashi is a Japanese manga artist and the creator of Naruto. His other famous work is Samurai 8 and Boruto. Masashi was born in Okayama Prefecture, Japan on November 8th, 1974. Pierrot is a Japanese animation studio made in May 1979. It was made former employees of Tatsunoko and Mushi Production. Pierrot is known for many famous anime like Naruto, Bleach, Black Clover, and Tokyo Ghoul.

Music Composer

Yasuhara is Japanese music composer and he from Tokyo, Japan and was born on April 13th, 1963. Yasuhara composes in animes ann video games. Some of his famous works are in Naruto, Fairy Tail, Sailor Moon Crystal, and Beyblade G-Revolution.
