Favorite Foods

Chicken Biryani

This food is chicken biryani and it is a mixed rice dish. This dish originated amongst Muslims and Indians. It is made with rice, spices, chicken and sometimes vegetables or eggs.

Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken is prepared by marinating chicken with yogurt and spices, then grill or bake chicken to cook. This dish orginated around India and is very popular all over the world. Tandoori chicken is usally eaten with biryani.

Jalebi

Jalebi is made by deep-frying maidi flour batter, then it is soaked in sugar syrup. This food orginated in India can can be served warm or cold. This is a dessert and has many different names, but I call it Zeelaafi.

Pizza

Every pizza has dough, cheese and sauce, but you can add more toppings and ingredients if you like. Pizza tastes best when cooked in a brick oven, this allows for some parts of the pizza to be burnt, which makes the pizza tatses better.

Favorite Apex Characters

Bangalore came out at the start of Apex and is a free character. Her passive allows her to run faster when an opponent is shooting at her. Her tactical ability releases an area of smoke and her ultimate ability calls an air strike.
