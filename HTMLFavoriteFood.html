Favorite Food #1: Chicken Biryani

This food is chicken biryani and it is a mixed rice dish. This dish originated amongst Muslims and Indians. It is made with rice, spices, chicken and sometimes vegetables or eggs.

Why I like this food

  1. My mom makes this during specail occasions
  2. There many different types of biryani
  3. Different cultures have different varients of this dish

Favorite Food #2: Tondoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken is prepared by marinating chicken with yogurt and spices, then grill or bake chicken to cook. This dish orginated around India and is very popular all over the world. Tandoori chicken is usally eaten with biryani.

Why I like this food

  1. This dish goes great with biryani
  2. It taste better when cooked on the grill
  3. It is very well seasoned

Favorite Food #3:Jalebi

Jalebi is made by deep-frying maidi flour batter, then it is soaked in sugar syrup. This food orginated in India can can be served warm or cold. This is a dessert and has many different names, but I call it Zeelaafi. There is a popular song about Jalebi and it is called Jalebi Baby.

Why I like this food

  1. I eat this dish every night during Ramadaan
  2. I like Jalebi when it cold
  3. It tastes great with biryani because it is a combintion of sweet, spicy, and salty
